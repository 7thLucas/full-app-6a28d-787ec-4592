# Product Overview — Hello QuantumByte

## What It Is
A minimal single-page landing application with one section displaying a bold "Hello QuantumByte" welcome message. Designed as the cleanest, most direct entry point for the QuantumByte brand — one page, one purpose.

## Users
Anyone arriving at the QuantumByte web presence: stakeholders, collaborators, curious visitors, or first-time encounters with the brand.

## Positioning
The simplest possible QuantumByte presence. No distractions, no clutter — just the brand name delivered with confidence. A digital handshake that speaks clearly from the first pixel.

## Brand & Tone
- **Brand name**: QuantumByte
- **Tone**: Clean, confident, welcoming
- **Visual direction**: Minimal, modern — bold typography, dark-on-light, indigo/violet accent palette

## Scope
- 1 landing page
- 1 section
- Primary message: "Hello QuantumByte"

## Strategic Principles
- **Simplicity first** — one clear message is more powerful than ten scattered ones
- **Speed** — instant load, zero bloat, nothing unnecessary
- **Brand clarity** — QuantumByte front and center at all times
