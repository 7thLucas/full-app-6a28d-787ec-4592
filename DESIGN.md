# Design Guidelines — Hello QuantumByte

## Colors
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or near-white off-white (#F9F9FB)
- Primary text: Near-black (#111118)
- Accent: Indigo/Violet (#6366F1 to #7C3AED range)
- Muted/secondary text: Slate gray (#64748B)

## Typography
- Font family: System sans-serif stack or Inter — clean, geometric, highly legible
- Hero heading: Extra-large, bold weight (700–900), tight letter-spacing
- Tone: Confident and modern

## Elevation & Layout
- Single full-viewport-height hero section (100vh)
- Content centered both vertically and horizontally
- Minimal padding, generous whitespace
- No navigation, no footer — pure hero focus

## Components
- Hero Section: Full-screen centered layout
  - Main heading: "Hello QuantumByte" in large bold type
  - Optional subtle accent line or underline in indigo/violet
  - Clean, distraction-free background

## References (Apple HIG spirit)
- Clarity: Typography does the heavy lifting
- Deference: Background recedes, content leads
- Depth: Minimal — flat design, maybe subtle gradient or shadow on text only
